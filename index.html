Care & shipping.

Care of the glass

Hand wash only, in lukewarm water with a soft cloth. Never a dishwasher, never abrasives. Dry upside down on linen.

Keep clear of direct heat and of other glass — the rostrato points are sharper than they look, to you and to each other.

glass remembers. treat it gently.
Shipping

Every piece is packed by the maker in moulded stone-wool and double-walled board, then shipped worldwide, fully insured, within 5 business days of your order.

Shipping is free over $400 AUD. Australia arrives in 2–4 days; the rest of the world in 5–10. You'll receive tracking the moment it leaves the studio.

Returns

Fourteen days, no questions. A one-of-one piece returned in original condition goes quietly back into the catalogue — its form is only retired once it stays sold.

If a piece arrives broken, we refund in full. We can't remake it — there is no mould — but we will make you first in line for what comes next.

Gift cards

Issued on paper, posted in an envelope, for any amount. They don't expire — the right piece may not exist yet.
